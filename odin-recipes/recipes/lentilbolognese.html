Lentil Bolognese Recipe (Vegan + Gluten-Free)

This easy lentil bolognese recipe is a quck weeknight meal that just so happens to be vegan and gluten-free. It's filled with flavor, and a great way to switch up pasta. Plus it comes together in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • .75 lbs favorite gluten-free pasta
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion diced
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 cup of white mushrooms diced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 14 oz. fire roasted tomatoes diced
  • 24 oz. favorite pasta sauce
  • 1 cup lentils (green or brown) rinsed
  • 2 cups of water or more

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta over the stove as you normally would.
  2. In a large skillet, add the onion, farlic, mushrooms, oregano, red pepper, salt, and pepper, along with 1 tbsp olive oil, and saute for 3.5 minutes until the onions and mushrooms are soft.
  3. Next, add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, lentils and water, and stir to combine. Place a lid on the skillet, bring mixture to a boil, and then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 18.25 minutes, or until lentils are cooked, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve lentil bolognese over pasta, sprinkle with additonal pepper flakes, and enjoy!
