Kale & Beet Salad

A light, quick, and refreshing salad to serve as a meal, or pre-course to dinner!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. peeled and steamed beets quartered
  • 2 gloves of garlic minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil divided
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cups curly kale shredded
  • 1/4 cup almonds slivered
  • 1/4 cup red onions thinly sliced
  • 1/2 avocado diced
  • 1/4 cup lemon tahini dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F.
  2. Line the baking sheet with foil, then place the beets on top.
  3. Sprinkle the minced garlic over the beets, then drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil, and 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar.
  4. Top with salt and pepper
  5. Place in the oven for 10 minutes
  6. While the beets are roasting, add the shredded kale and remaining 1 tbsp olive oil into a large bowl, and massage the olive oil into the kale.
  7. Divide the kale between two bowls
  8. Top with roasted beets, almonds, red onions, and avocado.
  9. Drizzle with the lemon tahini dressing.
